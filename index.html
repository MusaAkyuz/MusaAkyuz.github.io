-> Goal

The Fart Project is a rocket project whose goal is to reach the Kármán Line. The Kármán Line is the boundary between Earth's atmosphere and outer space, located at an altitude of about 100 kilometers (62 miles) above sea level. The side goal is to finish the project (hahaha) and share all the information I gained on this journey. Like the Kármán Line, which I Googled 20 seconds ago, I will share technical stuff, sources, books I’ve read, papers, people I’ve talked to, experiments... etc.
